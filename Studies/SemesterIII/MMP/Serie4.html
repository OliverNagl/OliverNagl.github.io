\( \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} \int_0^1 \frac{n^a x}{1+ n^b x^2 } e^{-x^2} \)


1) \( f_n \in L^1 \) für alle \( n \in \mathbb{N} \) 2) \( \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} \frac{n^a x}{1+ n^b x^2 } e^{-x^2} = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} \frac{x}{\frac{1}{n^a}+ \frac{n^b x^2}{n^a}} e^{-x^2} = \)
\( f_n = \frac{n^a x}{1+ n^b x^2 } e^{-x^2} < \frac{n^a x}{n^b x^2} = \frac{n^a}{n^b x} \)


\(\blacksquare \)
