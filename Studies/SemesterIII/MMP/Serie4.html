Aufgabe 1a:


\( \to_{n \rightarrow \infty} \int_0^1 \frac{n^a x}{1+ n^b x^2 } e^{-x^2} dx \)


1) \( f_n \in L^1 \) für alle \( n \in \mathbb{N} \)
2) \( \to_{n \rightarrow \infty} \frac{n^a x}{1+ n^b x^2 } e^{-x^2} = \to_{n \rightarrow \infty} \frac{x}{\frac{1}{n^a}+ \frac{n^b x^2}{n^a}} e^{-x^2} = 0 := f(x) \)
3)
\( f_n = \frac{n^a x}{1+ n^b x^2 } e^{-x^2} < \frac{n^a x}{n^b x^2} = \frac{n^a}{n^b x} < x^{-1} := g(x) \in L^1 \)


Somit mittels DCT \( \to_{n \rightarrow \infty} \int_0^1 \frac{n^a x}{1+ n^b x^2 } e^{-x^2} dx = 0 \)

\(\blacksquare \)
